## 🎯 Overview

The Tattoo Autoclave Cycle & Safety Calculator is a specialized tool for ensuring the highest standards of sterilization in professional studios. It helps artists and owners calculate necessary cycle times based on temperature and pressure variants, ensuring equipment is safe for use and compliant with health regulations.

**Key Use Cases:**

- Sterilization cycle verification
- Time/Temperature/Pressure correlation
- Safety protocol logging assistance

**Target Audience:**

- Tattoo Artists
- Piercing Professionals
- Studio Owners
- Health Inspectors
